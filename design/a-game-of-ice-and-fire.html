A Game of Ice and Fire

A Game of Ice and Fire is a two-player platform game designed to help children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) by encouraging communication, cooperation, and planning during gameplay, thus targeting the social skills that ASD often impairs. It was developed using GameMaker Studio, with the help of 3 other students, for our ENGR 100.650 class.
