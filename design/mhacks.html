MHacks

MHacks is one of the largest hackathons in the nation. The MHacks design team works to design a logo, app, website, and promotional materials for each of our events.

As a director of MHacks, I oversaw the design team for MHacks X. As the design lead, I oversaw and worked directly with the team for MHacks 9. For MHacks 8, I worked on the design team, with two other designers.

Learn more about MHacks at mhacks.org.
